# Foundation Requirements Meeting — Questionnaire (24 Q)

Purpose: confirm the real end-to-end operating model before we build the dispatch screens.
Gates the domain work in [BRIDGE-PLAN.md](BRIDGE-PLAN.md) §7. Bring the answers back → GSD discuss/spec/plan.

## A. The end-to-end flow (confirm the model)
1. Walk us through **one real rescue, start to finish** — who does what, in what order?
2. Who **flags** that a restaurant has surplus — the restaurant itself, or your team calls/checks in?
3. Is it mostly **event/wedding leftovers**, or also **daily** restaurant surplus?
4. Who does the **food safety/quality check**, where, and when (before pickup / at pickup)? Can a pickup be **rejected**?

## B. Roles & people
5. Who are the **drivers** — paid staff, hired rickshaws, or volunteers? How many active?
6. Do drivers have **smartphones + data**, and will they keep an app **open during a trip**?
7. What do **volunteers** do exactly — ride with the driver, or wait at the drop zone to distribute?
8. Who is the **dispatcher** (assigns drivers) — one coordinator, or several?

## C. Restaurants / partners
9. Do restaurants want to use an **app/portal themselves**, or should your team **log everything** for them?
10. How are restaurants **onboarded/vetted**? Roughly how many partner restaurants?

## D. Recipients / zones
11. Where does food go — fixed **slum/roadside zones**, shelters, or wherever there's need that day?
12. Is there a known **list of recurring drop zones**? How many, and which areas?
13. Who **confirms** the food was distributed, and do you count **people/families served** (headcount)?

## E. Tracking & visibility
14. Who needs to **watch the live location** — only the dispatcher, or also restaurants/donors?
15. OK that live tracking works **only while the driver keeps the app open** (web limit — phone-locked/backgrounded stops it)? True background tracking needs a **native app** later.
16. Do you need **ETA** ("driver 8 min away"), or just "where is the driver now"?

## F. Scale & context
17. How many **rescues/pickups per day** now, and the target in 6–12 months?
18. What **devices** do your team/drivers/volunteers use most (Android phones? a laptop)?
19. **Dominant language** for the people using the app — Gujarati, Hindi, English? Mix?

## G. Comms, money, brand, priorities
20. Do you need **SMS/WhatsApp** alerts (costs money in India), or are in-app + email enough for now?
21. Accept **online monetary donations** (Razorpay, ~2% fee) + issue **80G** receipts? (needs NGO KYC — long lead time, start early)
22. Can you share **real impact numbers, photos, brand assets** to replace placeholders? (logo colors already captured → green + gold)
23. **Top 3 must-haves** for v1 — if we ship only three things, what are they?
24. Any **budget** for paid services (SMS, Google Maps, a real domain, a native app), or strictly free for now?
